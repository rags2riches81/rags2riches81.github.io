ABD Founder and Artistic Director Anne Bluethenthal has been a pioneer in socially engaged art for over three decades. She has built a deceptively subversive repertoire deeply rooted in feminist, anti-colonial, and anti-patriarchal methodology. And while creating over 200 performance works, her methodology and choreographic vision have been enormously influential on at least two generations of SF dancers.
